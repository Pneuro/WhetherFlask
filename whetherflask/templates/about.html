Born in 1986, growing up I had always had an interest, passion and use for computer. My first love has been music, though the fury was fed through computers. The Python programming language has been a great teacher of logical thinking, idea mapping and problem solving, and I just cannot find enough.

With this in mind, I've created this Flask based website to share some of my previous creations, and to hopefully share a few more. Thanks for stopping by, and please contact me if you're interested in working together. I am a self-learner. I enjoy teaching myself new concepts, stumbling across new philosophies and challenging myself and others to do the same.

Dylan has been playing music since the age of 8 and his obsession with technology and solving complex problems makes him the quintessential music theory guru and self proclaimed nerd of the group. He has been a performing musician for most of his life and formerly was an instructor at School of Rock in the Cleveland, Ohio area. He is a certified Audio Engineer, owns a laundromat, and is a freelance Producer and Engineer at Sand Road Studios. Interests: Music, Technology, Business, Gaming and Exploration.
